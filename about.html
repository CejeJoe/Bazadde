About

Our About

Bazzade care

We are all about spreading hope, love and knowledge in families through educating and training under resourced parents on ways to live, and make money so that they can take care of their families.

Our Mission

To equip under resourced Ugandan parents holistically with spiritual, vocational, and financial resources to realize their potential and discover their worth.

Our Vision

To empower and encourage parents from under resourced families in Uganda to pursue a Christ-informed and Scripture based life style, while pursuing practical education in order to lead a life of dignity and self reliance.
