Providing opportunities in Vocational

Life skills training

literacy, numeracy, Biblical discipleship, goal setting, financial counseling and budgeting.

Funding for Vocational training

Each parent chooses a path for training. Vocational training is then offered via an accredited vocational school or apprenticeship in areas such as hairdressing, tailoring, sewing and auto mechanics.

Job Coaching

upon successful completion of training, HLK-Bazadde Care staff members continue to mentor parents to encourage growth in workplace skills such as interpersonal relationships, employment communication, leadership, problem solving, time management, decision making, workplace etiquette.

Small Business Grants

The final piece of the program is to assist parents as they establish a successful long term income position. Because many parents will choose to start a small business, HLK-Bazadde Care offers small business grants to provide start-up capital.
